CONSTRUCTION IS

A NUMBERS GAME

From tolerances to estimates, you need a team that can handle every aspect of coordination.

MATT Construction knows how to bring your building to life on time and

on budget. That pledge is built into every part of what we do.

Every stage in the life of a building, from its earliest designs to its grand unveiling, involves numerous moving parts. How does a top general contractor keep things on track? We engage as early as possible, typically during the conceptual or schematic stage.

Cost Control

We help to create reports on constructability and feasibility. Our comprehensive estimates also reflect available lower-price options, and we provide possible cost impacts and savings on a line item breakdown.

This frees our designers and clients to focus the budget on what's critical to them.

We thrive on helping owners

take control and make

informed decisions early.

TECHNOLOGY

Technology is reshaping the design field and what can be accomplished on a jobsite. We take full advantage of Building Information Modeling (BIM) to virtually construct interactive images of a project before we begin any in-the-ground efforts. Early 3D and 4D modeling enables us to sequence work appropriately, navigate site logistics, communicate with the design team and subcontractors, check for any potential systems conflicts and model the structural systems for fabrication.
